An Indian Passport is issued by the Indian Ministry of External Affairs to Indian citizens for the purpose of international travel. It enables the bearer to travel internationally and serves as proof of Indian citizenship as per the Passports Act (1967).

Types of Passport in India

1. Blue Passport :
Blue passports are generally issued to the general population.
2. White Passport :
White passports are issued to individuals representing the Indian government in official business.
3. Maroon Passport :
A diplomatic passport is issued to Indian diplomats, top-ranking government officials and diplomatic couriers. Officials like IPS (Indian Police Services), IAS (Indian Administrative Services) can apply for diplomatic passports.
